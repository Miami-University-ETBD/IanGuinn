About D&D

1974 In the year 1974 Dungeons and Dragons was created by Gary Gygax, this creation would change the nerd community forever.

Dungeons and Dragons (D&D) is a table top role playing game, TTRPG, about high fantasy settings with elves and dwarves adventuring fighting demons and saving the world.
