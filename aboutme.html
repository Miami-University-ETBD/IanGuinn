About Me

August 26, 2016: The day I moved up from Texas was, at the time, the worst day of my life. It felt like my world fell apart and I couldn't hold it together.

It wasn't until I made friends and started to understand that it wasn't a halt on my life, but a new beginning that I started to live again and spread my wings. I developed new skills and learned new hobbies to make my life here easier. I started playing D&D regularly and that also helped deepen my stroy writing goals.

The Evolution of my Dreams

My dreams took a turn in multiple stages of my life, these include:

  • Learning D&D for the first time
  • Discovering PC gaming
  • Read "The Hobbit"
  • Trying to DM for a game for the first time

These individual instances helped froge that is the steel trap of my fantasy knowledge. It was crafted and is what I use in every game I play and create.
