About Me

August 25, 2002: I was born, the earth shook and I alone stood still. No but for real, I was born in Texas and lived 14 years of my life there. I learned to walk, talk and grew into a young man in those hot summers. Though, it wasn't until I moved to Hamiltion, Ohio that I learned what it was to be loved by another, trusted by friends and even the stong of betrayal.

When I came to Ohio I was mad and confused. My mom took me from my friends and what I knew. I was alone and sad. I eventually found friends and learned how to live in the new enviornment.

My Dreams and Accolades

As long as I can remember I have wanted to write worlds and create stories, Dungeons & Dragons, The Hobbit and Cyberpunk have played a big role in upsizing my story/world building mindset. My designs include:

  • The Regicides: D&D Campaign Setting
  • Aurora City of Dreams: Cyberpunk RED Setting
  • Igrimel: Descent into Chaos (D&D World)
  • Equivilant Exchange: Alchemic Card Game

Not all of these projects have seen total completion but they are being worked on or have been delayed due to scheduling issues.
